Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Mission Statement We believe in privacy by design. We do not collect, store, share, or publish your personal data. Your digital life belongs to you.

1. Introduction & Scope

Moonforge Arcade LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy applies to our mobile applications (including AEROVOID) and our website. By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data Collection Practices

To ensure transparency, we strictly limit data interaction:

  • No Direct Collection: We do not operate servers that harvest user profiles, emails, or behavioral tracking.
  • Local Storage: Game progress, settings, and high scores are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this data is removed.
  • Crash Reporting: We may receive anonymous, aggregate crash logs via platform providers (Apple/Google) to identify software defects. These logs do not contain personally identifiable information (PII).

3. Third-Party Service Providers

While Moonforge Arcade LLC does not collect data, our Services are distributed via third-party platforms that may collect data under their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the data practices of:

  • Apple App Store & Game Center: Subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services: Subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our Services are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately for deletion.

5. International Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Even though we do not collect personal data, we respect your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: You may inquire about the categories of data we process (which is effectively none).
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of data (though we hold no data to delete).
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
